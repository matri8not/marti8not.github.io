Mission and vision

The Green Accelerator will help Macedonian companies address the key barriers to enter new markets by providing technical assistance, research support, academic stаff and financial support to pay for the services and related hardware required to complete the export order.

Our greening accelerator program is supported by the Faculty of Technology and Metallurgy within Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje, with their research laboratories and academic staff, we want to transform our waste into reusable and useful recycled products.

The purpose of the Accelerator is to increase Macedonian green exports. More specifically, it will support the preparatory work for green projects with export potential, but which are yet to find the right structure and format.

Accelerate Green Export

Our Green Accelerator will contribute for the companies to find finance i) preparatory business activities and ii) secondments and short term visits by advisers and experts, including the posting of financial advisers to selected Macedonian representations abroad in order to support the maturation of projects with a potential for Macedonian exports.

The overall objective of our Green Export Accelerator is to provide financial support to already mature Macedonian export solutions, thereby supporting Macedonian companies in their final push to enter new foreign markets. The expected impact is the attainment of new export orders and creation of new greening business jobs.
