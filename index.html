About us

The Macedonian Green Export Accelerator

is an initiative launched in May 2023 by two greening futurists, Mrs. Anita Koteska, an expert in sustainability and greening businesses with a global perspective, green tech oriented, and Mr. Martin Pejkov, a nature conservation futurist, biodiversity and green tech futurist.

The main idea

of our first step

of “greening business ideas” was to implement green transformation in numerous businesses and to recycle and reuse Macedonian waste in green export products with an additional value. We are accelerating ideas, transforming waste and other business processes into a real green business. Our first project for recycling and reusing waste tires was granted by the FITD (Fund for Innovation and Technology Development).
